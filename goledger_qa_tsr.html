Conclusion: The system presents 4 critical security vulnerabilities — authentication bypass by password length, unauthenticated blockchain asset deletion, plaintext password exposure, and persistent sessions after logout. Beyond security, BUG-008 represents a ledger integrity failure: tenant assignments are silently not written to the Hyperledger Fabric ledger, verified directly via CCAPI readAssetHistory. The Libraries and Persons modules are functional. The system is not recommended for production until critical and high-severity bugs are resolved.
